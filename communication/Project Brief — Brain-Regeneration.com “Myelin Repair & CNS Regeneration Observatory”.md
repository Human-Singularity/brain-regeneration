# Project Brief — Brain-Regeneration.com “Myelin Repair & CNS Regeneration Observatory” {#project-brief-—-brain-regeneration.com-“myelin-repair-&-cns-regeneration-observatory”}

## Summary {#summary}

[**Gregory MS**](https://Gregory-MS.com?utm_source=project-brief) is our current project that gathers all the relevant research in the field of Multiple Sclerosis. Our goal is to expand it and **help speed up research into Brain Regeneration.**

Gregory-MS is a 100% donor-funded, and uses an open-source AI platform created in 2019 after founder Bruno Amaral’s multiple sclerosis (MS) diagnosis. Gregory continuously harvests and de-duplicates scientific literature and clinical-trial records, applies ML models to rank relevance, and pushes tailored alerts to researchers and patients within hours of publication. The live MS Observatory already tracks 48 drugs/therapies and a dedicated stem-cell channel. [https://gregory-ms.com/observatory/](https://gregory-ms.com/observatory/?utm_source=project-brief)

The next phase is to build a **knowledge hub** that:

- Provides real-time tracking of published research and clinical trials;  
- maps every therapeutic avenue able to reverse damage caused by MS, Alzheimer’s Disease, Parkinson’s and other neuro-degenerative diseases (e.g., remyelination, neuroinflammation control, stem-cell approaches);  
- lists and monitors associated clinical trials for these conditions;  
- continues Gregory’s real-time alerting for investigators;  
- provides patient-centric views highlighting practical impact.

We are looking for strategic partners and senior scientific advisors to steer scope, validate curation criteria, and amplify reach through their networks.

## 4\. Partnership model {#4.-partnership-model}

The options for partnership with the project are still in debate and depend on the availability of patterns and the possibility of obtaining funding.

- **Role flexibility:** from light-touch scientific mentorship (quarterly calls) to deeper co-development.  
- **In-kind contribution welcomed:** advisory time, domain expertise, data access, dissemination.  
- **Reciprocity:** free customised access to Gregory, co-branding on dashboards/newsletters, visibility in press communications.  
- **No financial ask** at present; operating costs covered by donations. Sustainability roadmap open for discussion.

## Potential partner questions {#potential-partner-questions}

1. **Platform & data**

   - What are the primary data sources, and how often are they refreshed?

     - There are 20 data sources in use right now, with the option to add more. An up-to-date list of sources and query details is available at [https://gregory-ms.com/sources/](https://gregory-ms.com/sources/)  
   - How is the AI relevance score validated? Are false-positive rates published?

     - We do not have any team member with knowledge of neurology to validate our training of the Machine Learning models. The current data set was annotated by Bruno Amaral and the algorithms that are in place were developed by [Nova SBE](https://www.novasbe.unl.pt/en/)  
   - Can we export data into our own BI tools or EHR systems?

     - Yes, the API is open, and other means to export the data can be arranged.  
   - What security and GDPR safeguards are in place?

     - We store as little data as possible from the subscribers, and use a privacy-friendly analytics system that does not use cookies or store any Personal Identifiable Information (PII)  
     - Our analytics can be viewed and audited at this link: [https://analytics.gregory-ms.com/share/bEuESUpF/GregoryMS](https://analytics.gregory-ms.com/share/bEuESUpF/GregoryMS)

   

2. **Governance & IP**

     - The code is available on GitHub [https://github.com/brunoamaral/gregory-ai](https://github.com/brunoamaral/gregory-ai)  
   - Who retains ownership of the derivative analytics we create?

     - We aim to be an open-science project  
   - How is long-term funding secured beyond donations?

     - 

   - What happens if the project is spun off into a separate domain?

     - The purpose of securing the domain brain-regeneration.com was to make the project as broad as possible and avoid the need to spin off in a new direction. If that need does come up, it will have to be discussed with all the partners.

   

3. **Engagement & expectations**

   - What is the minimum advisory time commitment?

     - We have not set any minimum commitment as we are in an exploration phase
