[Lisbon Collective](https://lisboncollective.com/) is a digital consultancy founded by Bruno Amaral, specialising in digital communication strategy, UX research, and AI implementation.

Lisbon Collective is the originating organisation behind the Gregory-MS project — the research aggregation platform that grew into the Brain Regeneration Observatory. It contributes to the project in several ways:

- **Technical expertise** — design, development, and ongoing maintenance of the GregoryAi platform, including its research aggregation pipeline, AI-assisted classification system, and web infrastructure.
- **Infrastructure and hosting** — Management of the servers and services that keep the Observatory running.
- **Digital strategy and communication** — supporting outreach, partnerships, and the Observatory's public presence.
- **Financial backstop** — covering operational expenses when project donations are insufficient to meet costs.

Lisbon Collective's involvement in the Brain Regeneration Observatory reflects a long-term commitment to open science infrastructure and to making research accessible to patients, caregivers, and clinicians beyond the academic world.
