Why your support matters

The Brain Regeneration Observatory is free for everyone — patients, caregivers, clinicians, and researchers. There are no paywalls, no ads, and no commercial sponsors. That independence is by design, but it depends on the generosity of people like you.

Your donation helps cover the costs of running the platform: server infrastructure, AI processing, data harvesting, and the ongoing development of GregoryAI — the open-source engine behind the observatory.

What your donation supports

  • Infrastructure — Servers, databases, and APIs that power the platform
  • AI development — Improving the algorithms that find and rank relevant research
  • Data coverage — Expanding the sources and conditions we monitor
  • Open access — Keeping everything free and available to those who need it
