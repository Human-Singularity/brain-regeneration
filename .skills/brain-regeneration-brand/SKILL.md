# Brain Regeneration Observatory Brand

## Overview

This skill defines the lean brand identity for Brain-Regeneration.com, a knowledge hub that uses AI to accelerate research into brain regeneration and bring grounded hope to patients living with neurological diseases. The brand bridges two audiences: researchers who need precision, and patients who need clarity and trust.

For complete specifications including colour values, typography, imagery, and voice rules, see `references/brand-spec.md`.

## Core Identity

### Mission statement

We use AI to surface the science that matters, so researchers can move faster and patients can stay informed.

### Values

- Transparency and openness
- Recognition of merit
- Hope grounded in evidence
- Innovation in how science reaches people

### Brand personality

Grounded, warm, precise, forward-looking. Never hype. Never cold.

## Quick Reference

### Colours

- Primary: Deep Teal `#1A6B6A` — trust, depth, calm authority
- Secondary: Warm Sand `#F5F0E8` — approachability, warmth, readability
- Accent: Signal Amber `#E8913A` — hope, energy, calls to action
- Text Dark: `#2D2D2D` — primary body text
- Text Light: `#FFFFFF` — text on dark backgrounds
- Muted Grey: `#8C8C8C` — secondary text, metadata, timestamps
- Light Teal: `#E6F2F2` — hover states, tags, highlighted blocks
- Border: `#D9D4CA` — card borders, dividers, table lines

### Typography (all free/open-source)

- Headings: Inter (sans-serif, clean, modern)
- Body: Source Serif 4 (readable, warm, trustworthy)
- Data/code: JetBrains Mono (precision, technical contexts)
- Fallbacks: system sans-serif, Georgia, monospace

### Voice

Default to plain language. Use precise terminology but always make it accessible. Write for a reader who is intelligent but not necessarily a specialist.

## When to Apply

Use this skill any time you produce content for the Brain Regeneration Observatory:

- Hugo page templates and partials
- Homepage and landing page copy
- Research area descriptions
- Curator profiles
- Presentations or pitch decks
- Documents for partners or donors
- Email templates and newsletter copy
- Social media content

Always load `references/brand-spec.md` for the complete specification before creating any branded output.
